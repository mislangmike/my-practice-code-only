Equations Used:

1. Time of Flight (T):

T = (u * sin(θ) + √((u * sin(θ))² + 2gh)) / g

2. Maximum Height (H):

H = h + (u² * sin²(θ)) / (2g)

3. Horizontal Range (R):

R = (u² * sin(2θ)) / g

4. Velocity at Time (v):

v = u * cos(θ) - gt
